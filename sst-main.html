Welcome to Scooby's Sweet Treats!

Scooby's Sweet Treats is a Wedding Hire business available throughout all of Cornwall specialising in amazing feats and sweet treats.

We are a small family run business based in North Cornwall. Our aim is to go above and beyond to make your event as special as can be. Attention to detail is key and expectations need not be managed.

Our hot-ticket items are the handmade Victorian-style and traditional rustic-style candy carts. This versatile display furniture can be used however you intend and provide any special occasion with an elegant and memorable aesthetic.

Food Hygiene

Fighting crime and grime

Scooby's Sweet Treats is a food registered business with Cornwall Council. We have full public and product liability insurance and hold a level 2 in food hygiene in catering with food allergy training. We take every possible measure to ensure completely sanitary evnvironments.

HACCP Approved

Sweet, sweet safety

Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points, or HACCP, is a systematic preventive approach to food safety from biological, chemical, and physical hazards. Every care is taken reduce these risks to a manageable level or factor them out entirely. Don't worry, we've got you covered!
